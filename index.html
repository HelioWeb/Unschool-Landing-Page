About us

Hi, let us share few things about our attempt to revisit the idea of preschool :)

Parents often ask us, “at preschool, would my child be learning enough for his age ?” Yes, as parent it is a basic expectation from a preschool. We think [pre]school can do little more than just “enough for the age” :)

We believe there is a unique way of customised learning, mainly practiced by homeschooling parents, that is capable of focusing on teaching to the strengths and the weaknesses of the individual child. We have noticed most of these practices are unknown to Indian parents, as it is more prominently practiced in more educationally evolved countries.

We have integrated such home schooling techniques with mainstream education. Here we do not design curriculum, instead we design days.

For kids between age of 1.5 to 6 along with “time in the school”, we have as well designed “time at home” filled with playful activities and sound observations, that could silently help build up characters. This also includes strong involvement from parents, so that together we can help the kid.

We have used this method of learning in India and Singapore, and we were able to find good results. Now we invite you to experience the same with us.

About Preschool

Let me introduce!
✕
How surprised would you be to know that the national education policy and basic frameowrk of teaching is 31 years old?
It was designed and planned around the time when Photo CDs were invented. Video CDs still did not exist. The irrelevance of that teaching pattern is like teaching a child how to use a calculator when they can actually make one.
Ofcourse there has been a few upgrades in the curriculum over the years, though the content of learning is not growing with the same speed as the technology. Also, there is a predecided amount of information to be given to the child as per their age. This ratio has not changed in all these years inspite of the kids being twice as adaptive since then. Education in India is the next big thing that could be remodelled.
As homeschooling parents, we have had a freestyle of teaching techniques combining a lot of different patterns of learning from fairly distinct communities. Now we think, we have gathered enough to start spreading it further and actually make a difference.
Setup
✕
We have built a tiny world for kids in a way that they feel belonged. Spread over the area of 3500 sqft and 3 different sections. We have the learning rooms, open play area and library.
  1. The learning rooms: We have spacious learning rooms where 2 teachers are involved with 10 kids in a batch. The rooms are interactive and as per the child safety measures. kids are encouraged to create a controlled mess and have their own flow of the space.
  2. Open play area : This section is purely for different types of play and making friends. There is a spread of educational wooden toys, intellectual puzzles and board games. This space also has a setup where parents can play along with the kids or just relax and work while kids play around. This area is open for our preschoolers as well as general admissions.
  3. Library : A place where kids have access to lots of books in various languages and tiny cozy corners for them to just have a great time with their books. They may pick their books and sit in a tent or sleep on a hammock or just lie down on grass and have a good read.
The entire place is design considering the accessibility and safety of children. We have specially trained every single member of our staff for first aid and fire hazard. We are more than prepared to welcome your kid to be a part of this tiny world of ours.
What is different?
✕
The knowledge we wish to deliver through this preschool is completely based on the aptitude of the kids today and their speed of learning.
There are a lot of topics that we wish to familiarise our kids with like racial harmony, world history, use of basic gadgets and technology, self defence and alertness, religious tolerance, Interantional cuisine, Table manners etc.
All the information that is not in any curriculum yet every parent would want their kids to know. We also focus on a lot of extracurricular involvement like music, instruments, dance, yoga, malkhamb, various sports, different form of fine arts, speech and drama, elocution.
We believe that kids develop an instinct about various activities from a very young age and the earlier we introduce them, the more clear they become of their passions and hobbies and likes and dislikes. We also wish to imapart some behavioural characteristics like replacement of gadgets, temper control, balance between discipline and freedom etc.
This is not just a model of teaching on paper but something we have been using over the years in the exact same format regularly while raising up kids. The outcome is so promising that this concept becomes a lot more than just fancy words.
Training Parents
✕
The whole idea of this preschool is to give kids a 24/7 exposure to learning and building a character. We can only accompany your child for a few hours but its the parents that they learn the most from by instinct. We wish to keep parents extensively involved in their growth. Dont worry, we wont take a lot of your time, just tiny things that makes a lot of difference.
  1. We invite parents to attend school with the kids once every week for our music together sessions where you can have a free play with the kid and transmit rhythm to the child with your body, or outings like forest play and industry visit.
  2. We would like to conduct monthly meetups where we can help you regarding dealing with the child at home according to their behavioural skills and unique learning pattern. We can discuss about the issues faced by the kid in general in their mental and personal growth and also understand their customised way of grasping information.
  3. We will provide you with weekly observation form. Your responses will help us understand child’s progress and receptiveness of the habits we are trying to build in the school.
  4. We would provide activities and games that child can play at home to keep them occupied as well as keep then constantly learn things while they think they are just playing. This will keep them away from excessive use of gadgets and feed their daily requirement mentally and physically.
We are looking forward to collaborate with you and benefit the kids in return.

Founder

I am a homeschooling mother of a 5 year old. Worked as a Paediatric Therapist and creative Curriculum Designer in Mumbai and Singapore.

My startup in Singapore is based on a concept, where kids learn through open ended toys. Toys that are filtered in 72 different growing capabilities, where we use toys as therapy.

Observing kids and their growth pattern very closely since my education years, I was NOT very clear on what I wanted to provide for my child, BUT was very sure what I wanted to avoid.

Thus seeded the idea of homeschooling. Where I can provide a customised learning for my child as per her strengths and shortcomings.

Using alternate curriculum and creative learning in parallel to main stream syllabus, I just banked on the concept of building an overall foundation in the growing years. I think the results are fascinating :) I earned my confidence when my kid at 5 had main stream syllabus understanding of age 9 and different levels of expertise in 5 languages, along with extra curricular involvement and free play.

Customised learning does make wonders!!

Along the way, I also came across a lot of parents, who were equally marvelled by the idea of this less practiced style of learning, but did not have the luxury to take the leap due to - personal, professional, social and economical commitments. That got me thinking.. What if we could create a midway where the same learning can be provided, but in a mainstream frame similar to a preschool. After-all, the main objective is to make a difference in a child growth.. right?
